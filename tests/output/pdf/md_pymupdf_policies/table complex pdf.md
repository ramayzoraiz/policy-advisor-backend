## ABOUT US 

Packages Limited was established in 1956 as a joint venture between Ali Group of Pakistan and Akerlund & Rausing of Sweden, and is renowned for providing premium packaging solutions. 

The Packages Group of companies are involved in the production, and sales of Folding Cartons, Flexible Packaging, Tissue Paper, Paper and board, Corrugated Packaging, BOPP & and CPP Films, Printing Inks, Corn-based Starches and industrial minerals derived from Calcium Carbonate, and Dolomite. The Group also holds investments in insurance, and pharmaceutical companies. Packages Group caters to the needs of various industries in Pakistan and abroad. 

It believes in transforming people’s lives for the greater good through its ethical practices, outstanding services, and quality products, ensuring all its stakeholders' satisfaction.

## CODE OF CONDUCT 

Packages Group’s code of conduct has been prepared to assist each one of us in our reports, to not only maintain but also enhance this reputation. It guides business conduct in several areas and references to more detailed corporate policies for further direction. The adherence of all employees to high standards of integrity and ethical behaviour is mandatory and benefits all stakeholders including our customers, communities, shareholders, and ourselves. 

The Company carefully checks for compliance with the Code by providing suitable information, prevention and control tools and ensuring transparency in all transactions and behaviours by taking corrective measures if and as required. 

Packages Convertors Limited Code of Conduct applies to affiliates, employees and others who act for us countrywide, within all sectors, regions, areas and functions. 

Our commitment to corporate governance is reflected in the following policies at Packages Group: 

**1. Sustainability Policy:** Packages Limited is dedicated to integrating sustainability into all aspects of our business models and decisions. Employees are expected to champion an ethical company culture that transcends mere regulatory compliance. All team members are empowered to implement and enhance this policy. 

**2. Equal Opportunity Policy:** At Packages Limited, a culture of fairness and nondiscrimination prevails. Employees are obligated to adhere to the equal opportunity policy, refraining from discrimination based on gender, marital status, age, disability, race, color, religion, or ethnic/national origin.
